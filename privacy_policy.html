Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Mortek ("we", "us", "our") operates the Crypto Mining Tycoon mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Crypto Mining Tycoon is a single-player game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

The App uses Google Play Games Services, which may collect data such as your Google account name, profile picture, and game scores for leaderboards and achievements. This data is handled by Google. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play. These transactions are processed entirely by Google. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles payment data.

Local Notifications

The App may send local notifications (e.g., reminders about offline earnings) if you grant notification permission. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing — for processing in-app purchases. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Games Services — for leaderboards and achievements. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Your game save data is stored in Google Play Games Services cloud save, linked to your Google account. This allows your progress to be restored if you reinstall the App or switch devices. We do not have direct access to this data — it is managed entirely by Google. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App requires a Google account to play, and Google Play Games Services may collect account data as described above. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please contact Google to manage their account data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
